I am a research scientist working at the intersection of computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on developing efficient and robust algorithms for real-world applications, with particular emphasis on deep learning, neural networks, and computational photography.

Research

My current research interests include neural networks, deep learning, low-level computer vision, inverse problems, computational photography and imaging. I am particularly interested in developing methods that can bridge the gap between theoretical advances and practical applications in real-world scenarios.
